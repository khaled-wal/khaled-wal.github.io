## The Challenge
The brand was suffering from weak organic reach and low interaction with followers, which negatively impacted sales volume from digital channels over the past year.

## The Strategy
We relied on three main pillars to improve performance:
1. **Producing authentic UGC content**: Partnering with micro-influencers to create content that looks natural and attracts the target audience.
2. **Precise targeting via Meta Ads**: Building ad campaigns based on user data and interests for retargeting and doubling conversions.
3. **A/B Testing analysis**: Continuous testing of images, copy, and CTAs to find what generates the best response.

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|---------|-----|-----|
| Monthly Reach | 12K | 89K |
| Engagement Rate | 1.2% | 4.8% |
| Digital Sales | 45K SAR | 153K SAR |
| ROI | — | **240%** |

Through these strategies, we were able to achieve results that far exceeded the campaign's pre-set goals.
