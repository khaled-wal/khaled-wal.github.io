## The Challenge

The e-commerce store was heavily reliant on paid advertising to attract visitors, driving up operational costs and making growth unsustainable in the long term.

## The Strategy

We built an integrated content strategy based on three pillars:
1. **Keyword Analysis**: Comprehensive keyword mapping based on search intent.
2. **High-Quality Content Production**: Educational articles and a specialised home design buying guide.
3. **Technical SEO Improvements**: Page speed optimisation, crawl error fixes, and smart internal linking.

## Results

| Metric | Before | After (6 months) |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Monthly Organic Visits | 3.2K | 7.8K |
| Keywords on Page 1 | 12 | 89 |
| Bounce Rate | 72% | 48% |
| Revenue from Organic Search | 15K SAR | 52K SAR |

The strategy achieved a radical shift in the traffic source mix, reducing reliance on paid advertising by 40%.
