## The Challenge

The brand lacked a clear and consistent visual identity, leading to poor recognition and scattered attention from potential customers across different channels.

## The Strategy

We adopted an integrated methodology to rebuild the identity:
1. **Market & Audience Research**: In-depth competitor analysis and identification of differentiators.
2. **Colour & Typography System**: Selecting a calm, cohesive palette aligned with target segments.
3. **Unified Brand Experience**: Applying the identity across all touchpoints (media kit, social platforms, packaging).

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Brand Recognition Rate | 12% | 58% |
| Social Media Engagement | 1.8% | 5.2% |
| Customer Retention Rate | 34% | 67% |

Through the new identity, the brand successfully penetrated a new age segment (18–28) and achieved notable growth in brand awareness.
