## Project Overview

I collaborated with a charitable association to produce a series of short-form videos (Reels & TikTok) that tell real stories in an impactful and emotional style, aiming to raise public awareness of the association's work and motivate audiences to participate and donate.

## Production Phases

### Pre-Production
Developing scripts, writing copy, and preparing a shooting plan within the available budget.

### Production
Filming real-life stories in authentic locations with attention to audio quality and natural lighting.

### Post-Production
Professional editing, adding expressive background music, and subtitles in both Arabic and English.

## Results
- Total reach exceeded 2.1 million views within two weeks.
- Donations via digital platforms increased by 85%.
- The association gained 12,000 new followers during the campaign period.
