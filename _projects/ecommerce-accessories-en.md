## Project Overview
This project aims to comprehensively restructure and develop a store specializing in electronic accessories. The work included improving the user interface and preparing an integrated digital marketing plan to increase brand awareness and achieve targeted sales growth.

## Visual Identity
The logo was updated, and a color palette was chosen to reflect technological advancement and appeal. In addition, social media post designs were standardized to ensure brand consistency.

## Marketing Campaigns
Paid advertising campaigns were executed via Google Ads and Meta Ads, targeting the age group (18-35) interested in technology.

## Content Creation
Produced visual and educational content highlighting product features and creative ways to use them.

## Results
- A significant increase in traffic by 45%.
- A 30% rise in monthly sales.
- Enhanced engagement on social media channels and steady growth in follower count.
